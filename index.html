Welcome to Palatable.

This project presents an exam scheduling application for graph-coloring-based algorithm, with the objective of achieving fairness, accuracy, and optimal exam time period. Through the work, we consider few assumptions and constraints, closely related to the general exam scheduling problem, and mainly driven from accumulated experience at University of Jordan. The performance of the algorithm is also a major concern of this project
